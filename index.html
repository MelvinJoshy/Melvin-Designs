Process

My Design Process

Research

This involves gathering information about the user, their needs, and the problem the design is trying to solve. The designer may use methods such as surveys, user interviews, and competitor analysis to gain insights.

Ideation

The designer brainstorms potential solutions and designs based on the research findings. This stage may involve sketching, wireframing, and creating mood boards

Prototyping

The designer creates a basic prototype of the design to test its usability and functionality. This may include creating a low-fidelity prototype using paper or digital tools, such as Adobe XD or Figma

Testing

The designer tests the prototype with users to gather feedback and insights. This stage may involve usability testing, A/B testing, or focus groups

Implementation

The final design is implemented and launched. The designer may continue to gather feedback and make improvements after launch
